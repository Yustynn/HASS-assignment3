Visualization of Percentage Price Increase for High Floor vs Low Floor in Various Towns

Red: Negative difference (low floor more valuable than high floor) | Blue: Positive difference (high floor more valuable than low floor)

This data was obtained by aggregating resale prices of flats from 2000-2012

In particular, the approximate procedure was:

  1. Filter to blocks with at least 5 low floor resales (below 7th storey)
  2. Filter to blocks with at least 5 high floor resales (above 13th storey)
  3. Find the ratio of (high floor resale mean) / (low floor resale mean) for flats in the same block sold on the same year
  4. Take the mean of these ratios for each town

For studying the effect of the flat type, a breakdown is provided by flat type as well.

To be honest, there are a lot of things difficult to control for, so do take this with a pinch of salt. E.g. didn't control for month-of-year effects, didn't weigh the mean by number of data points per block, etc etc.
